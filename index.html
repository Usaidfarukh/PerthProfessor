Meet the Professor

Hey Perth, I’m Andre Stokes — Perth’s Plumbing Professor.

Originally qualified in Ireland after a rigorous 5-year apprenticeship in Dublin, I’ve now clocked up over 20 years in the plumbing and gas game. I’ve worked across Ireland, Scotland (where I ran my own successful limited company, The Plumbing Professor Ltd), and for the last 11 years right here in Perth.

From commercial high-rises and tier-1 & 2 projects with builders like Adco, Multiplex and Pact, to private hospitals and large-scale civil works — I’ve done it all. I’ve led teams as a commercial supervisor, managed labour and materials, worked from complex drawings, and solved problems on site under tight deadlines.

After years working for big companies, I started Perth’s Plumbing Professor because I wanted to bring something different to WA homeowners, real estate agents and builders. I’m done with the usual tradie run-around.

I deliver premium, professional service with the knowledge and experience you’d expect from a supervisor who’s seen it all — but at honest, affordable prices with no call-out fee in business hours.

Whether it’s a blocked drain, a full bathroom renovation, gas servicing, real estate maintenance, or a new build, you deal directly with the owner. No call centres, no surprises, just straight-talking expertise and a guarantee the job is done right the first time.

That’s the Professor promise.
